about me

I received the BSc in Mathematics in 2008 and MSc degree in Applied Mathematics in 2011, both from the Instituto de Matemática — Universidade Federal de Alagoas, Brazil. I joined the Universidade Federal da Grande Dourados in 2015, where I am assistant professor at FACET. I am currently a PhD candidate at the Instituto de Ciências Matemáticas e de Computação (ICMC) — Universidade de São Paulo (USP), Brazil and member of Visual and Geometry Processing Group at ICMC — USP.

My research interests comprise interactive visualization for kernelized data.
