about me

I received the BSc in Mathematics in 2008 and MSc degree in Computer Graphics in 2011, both from the Instituto de Matemática — Universidade Federal de Alagoas and the PhD in Computer Graphics from the Instituto de Ciências Matemáticas e de Computação (ICMC) — Universidade de São Paulo (USP). I am member of Visual and Geometry Processing Group at ICMC — USP. I joined the Universidade Federal da Grande Dourados in 2015, where I am currently assistant professor at Faculdade de Ciências Exatas e Tecnologias.

My research interests comprise interactive visualization for kernelized data.
